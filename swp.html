SWP Calculation Formula
Year-by-Year Portfolio Balance:
Balance(n+1) = [Balance(n) × (1 + r)] - W(n)

Where:
Balance(n) = Portfolio balance at year n
r = Annual Return Rate (as decimal)
W(n) = Withdrawal in year n

Withdrawal Increase: W(n) = Initial Withdrawal × (1 + Increase Rate)^(n-1)
Sustainability Check: Plan is sustainable when annual withdrawals ≤ annual portfolio returns and portfolio lasts entire period.
