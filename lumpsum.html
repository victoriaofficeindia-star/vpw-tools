Lumpsum Calculation Formula
Basic Lumpsum Formula:
A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt)

Where:
A = Amount at Maturity
P = Principal (Initial Investment)
r = Annual Interest Rate (as decimal)
n = Compounding Frequency per year
t = Time in years

With Inflation Adjustment: Real value = Maturity Amount ÷ (1 + Inflation Rate)^Years
Compounding Frequency: Annually (1), Semi-Annually (2), Quarterly (4), or Monthly (12)
